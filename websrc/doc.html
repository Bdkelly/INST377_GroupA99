Documentation / How-to:

Finding a bus stop:

Step 1: Allow us to use your location by clicking the Get My Location button.

Step 2: Once we have your location, you can click on the Routes button to see which stops are closest to you.

Step 3: You should then see a line showing you the most direct route from your location to the nearest bus stop.

Step 4: If you want to see all the stops in the College Park area, click on the Get All Stops button.
